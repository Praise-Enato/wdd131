Edo State, Nigeria

Edo State is located in southern Nigeria and is known for its rich cultural heritage. It is home to the Benin Kingdom, one of the oldest and most developed states in West Africa. Edo State is famous for its arts and crafts, including the Benin Bronzes, and its vibrant festivals. The state capital, Benin City, is a hub of history and tradition.
