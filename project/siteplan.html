Site Name

NERISS HAIR CARE

  • This name clearly indicates the site's focus—wig care—while being simple and memorable.
  • Optional domain availability: neriss.com (example)

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an educational and practical resource for wig wearers, offering:

  • Step-by-step wig care guides (washing, styling, storage).
  • Product recommendations (shampoos, brushes, heat protectants).
  • Troubleshooting tips for common wig issues (tangling, shedding).
  • A community Q&A section (via a contact form).

Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

  • "How often should I wash my synthetic wig?" → Answered in the Cleaning Guide section.
  • "What's the best heat-resistant spray for human hair wigs?" → Featured in the Product Recommendations page.
